Pizza Bar Mozzarella
The Pizza Bar Mozzarella is a classic pizza featuring fresh

                        mozzarella cheese, tangy tomato sauce, and aromatic Italian herbs

                        on a crispy crust. Its harmonious blend of flavors makes it a favorite

                        choice for pizza enthusiasts, offering irresistible taste and texture

                        in every bite.
Ingredients / Steps
Ingredients
500 grams mozzarella cheese
10 slices mushrooms
3 bell peppers (assorted colors)
1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1 teaspoon cornmeal (for dusting)
Steps
1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, water, and oil for pizza dough.
2. Knead until smooth, let rise.
3. Shred cheese, slice mushrooms, peppers.
4. Roll out dough, add toppings.
5. Bake until crust is golden, Cool slightly, slice, and enjoy!
Prep Time
05 Mins
Cook Time
20 Mins
